Our Research

Dr. Catalina Vallejo-Giraldo’s research focuses on the intersection of biomaterials, brain mechanobiology, and medicine. She has received extensive international training, including academic experience at Universidad EIA in Colombia, Imperial College London, Cardiff University, and the University of Galway. Catalina has also gained industry experience at Neurent Medical in Ireland and clinical experience at the Mayo Clinic in the USA.

Her work includes the development of advanced biomaterials for neural interfaces and tissue engineering, with the aim of creating novel treatment modalities for neurological disorders, including Glioblastoma (GB).

Recently, she was awarded an ERC starting grant to develop a novel treatment modality for GB. Additionally, she serves as a referee for several international journals, is an early-career editorial board member of the FASEB Journal, and is a member of the Editorial Review Board for the Journal of Tissue Engineering (JTE).
