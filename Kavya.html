Kavyashree Nadig

Kavya completed her Bachelor’s degree with a triple major in Microbiology, Chemistry, and Zoology at St. Joseph’s University, Bangalore, followed by a Master’s in Biochemistry from the Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda, India. She earned her Ph.D. in Molecular Biophysics from the prestigious Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, India, where she investigated RNA-cleaving toxins and RNA–protein complexes using an interdisciplinary toolkit spanning biochemical assays, bioinformatics, large-scale RNA/protein purification, cryo-EM, and RNA-seq. A naturally cross-disciplinary thinker, she enjoys moving between techniques and concepts to uncover fresh perspectives on biological problems.
